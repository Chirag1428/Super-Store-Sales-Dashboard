# Super-Store-Sales-Dashboard

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is a comprehensive business intelligence solution that visualizes key metrics like total sales, profit, discounts, and shipping performance. It empowers retail managers to track performance by region, category, and customer segment for better inventory and marketing strategies.

Dashboard Insights :

1. Executive Summary – KPIs, total sales, profit trends

2. Regional Analysis – Map visuals and regional comparisons

3. Category & Product Analysis – Best/worst performing categories

4. Shipping Insights – Delivery speed and shipping cost impact

5. Customer Segment – Sales and profit by segment and customer base
